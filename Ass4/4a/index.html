JQuery Mobile

The page is the primary unit of interaction in jQuery Mobile and is used to group content into logical views that can be animated in and out of view with page transitions. A HTML document may start with a single "page" and the AJAX navigation system will load additional pages on demand into the DOM as users navigate around.
